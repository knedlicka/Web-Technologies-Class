Capri

Capri, an iconic island just off the coast, is famous for its striking landscapes, including the legendary Blue Grotto sea cave. Known as a luxurious getaway, Capri offers breathtaking panoramas, high-end boutiques, and historic Roman sites, making it a must-visit for those seeking a unique blend of nature, culture, and elegance.
